My Top 3 Movies

My top 3 movies of all time.

Interstellar

An epic science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. It tells the story of a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new home for humanity, as Earth is ravaged by a global famine caused by a catastrophic blight.

Baby Driver

The best movie that teaches that concepts of good and evil are not always clear-cut

Oppenheimer

It describes the life of Robert J. Oppenheimer the father of Amotic Bombs. It shows how human can posses things that have the potential to wipe out humanity from the face of the world.
